I got a new work laptop with 32GB of RAM, the highest-spec notebook I've ever used.

<!-- more -->

WSL on Windows has made great progress. Compared to two years ago when you had to tinker with various configurations, now you just need to prepare the network environment and quickly get it done using the wsl command line. The experience is much smoother.

As for development tools, I no longer use VSCode; instead, I switched to VSCodium. Without those annoying AI features, the interface is much cleaner—back to basics, so to speak.
